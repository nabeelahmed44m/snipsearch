# Privacy Policy for SnipSearch

**Effective Date:** March 14, 2026

At **SnipSearch**, we are committed to protecting your privacy while providing a powerful tool for code snippet management. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information when you use our Chrome Extension.

### 1. Information We Collect
SnipSearch is designed to store and search code snippets. As such, we collect:
*   **User-Generated Content:** The code snippets, text, and filenames you explicitly choose to upload to the SnipSearch database.
*   **Search Queries:** The search terms you enter to find your snippets.

We **do not** collect:
*   Browsing history or data from other websites.
*   Personally Identifiable Information (PII) like your name, address, or phone number (unless specifically provided via support email outside the app).

### 2. How We Use Information
We use the collected snippets and search queries solely to:
*   Provide the core functionality of the extension (storing and retrieving your code).
*   Generate AI embeddings (vectors) to enable semantic search capabilities.

**We do not sell, rent, or trade your data to third parties for marketing or any other purposes.**

### 3. Third-Party Services
To provide our service, we utilize the following trusted third-party providers:
*   **Vercel:** Used for hosting our backend API.
*   **Neon (Postgres):** Used for secure data storage and indexing.
*   **FastEmbed:** Used for generating AI embeddings locally or on our secure backend.

These providers are used only to process and store your data as required for the extension to function.

### 4. Data Security
We implement standard security measures (HTTPS encryption for all data in transit) to protect your code snippets. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

### 5. Data Retention & Deletion
Your snippets are stored until you choose to delete them (where the interface allows) or until you request deletion. 

### 6. Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy occasionally. We will notify users of any significant changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy.

### 7. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
