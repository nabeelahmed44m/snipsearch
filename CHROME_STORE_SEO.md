*   **Seamless Chrome Integration:** Access your entire snippet library without ever leaving your browser. Find, copy, and paste code directly into your IDE, PRs, or documentation.

*   **Instant Uploads:** Quickly add snippets, name them, and they are instantly embedded and saved into your searchable database.
